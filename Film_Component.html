Basic Camera

This is a basic film camera. It includes various different feautres that can be used in regards to a vast array of lighting and at the bare minimum they should have a stabalizer so you dont make your audience motion sick. Find out more on Film Camera Basics here.

Lighting and sound

Argueably more important that a decent camera is your lighting nd sound. Even with the most basic of cameras, with the right lighting, sound, and eye, you can make even the most basic of filming camera capture some rather professional shots. To learn more about lighting and sound, check this out!

All in one Hardware

This is a more accurate image of a professional cinema camera. As you can see these will be equiped with a manual stabalizer that can usually be hooked to a tool belt, a built in professional boom mic, often times lighting on cam, and a vast variety of different setting and adapters to be veratile with a lot of different equipment. These can range anywhere from $5,000 to $80,000 and they are the best fully rounded option for professional filmmakers, you can browse used professoinal cameras here.

Location Location Location!

There are many perameters of the film making process, but location management is one of the most important duties.

Here are some things to consider

The dutie of obtaining the right location for each scene is one thing that often gets overlooked in the flim planning process. As i'm sure you can imagine this is a fun, yet suprisingly difficult part of the film process that requires a lot of planning.


Typically there is a position called a PA or a production assistance. The PA is responsible for many things, to the director you can imagine that they are a nurse and the director is a Dr. While the director knows more and has the skill set, the PA generally does more and makes sure everything is running smooth.

One of the key responsibilities of the PA is location management. If we are filming a movie that has mountains in it, maybe a snowey scene, ect. The PA is responsible for scputing locations and booking them for the right time frame, depending on the time and location needed, the PA may need to get various city permits and contact seveal individuals for permission.
